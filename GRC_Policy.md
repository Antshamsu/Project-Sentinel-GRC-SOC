# Corporate Access Control Policy (v1.0)
**Alignment:** NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 (AC-2, AC-3)

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the requirements for managing user identities and access rights at [Mock Enterprise]. The goal is to ensure that access is granted based on the "Principle of Least Privilege" (PoLP) to minimize business risk.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors accessing corporate information systems.

## 3. Policy Statements
* **Identification:** All users must be uniquely identified. Shared accounts are strictly prohibited.
* **Authentication:** Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote and administrative access.
* **Account Monitoring:** Account activity must be logged and reviewed monthly (CISA Audit Standard).
* **Termination:** Access must be revoked within 24 hours of employee separation.

## 4. Business Impact & Risk (BBA Perspective)
Failure to enforce this policy results in:
1.  **Financial Risk:** Potential regulatory fines (GDPR/HIPAA).
2.  **Operational Risk:** Unauthorized data modification leading to business downtime.
3.  **Reputational Risk:** Loss of client trust following a credential-based breach.

## 5. Compliance Enforcement
Violations of this policy will be flagged by the SOC and investigated by the Security Analysis team.
